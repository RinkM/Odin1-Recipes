Morning After Oats

Makes 10 Mason Jars

Base Recipe:

  • 2.5 cups oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 3.25 cup milk
  • 2.5 cup yogurt

Apple Peanut Butter

  • 1 Apple, diced
  • 1 Tb Peanut Butter

Blueberry Coconut Lemon

  • 2 oz blueberries
  • 1 Tb Coconut Shavings
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice

Berry Mix

  • 2 ounces of your favorite berries

Strawberry Banana

  • 1 or 2 strawberries, sliced
  • 1 spoon of banana, sliced

Process

The recipe makes 10 jars worth, enough for 2 people for a work week. You'll need a large bowl to mix the oatmeal and liquids together.

Mix the ingredients to the base recipe in a large bowl. Any ingredient can be swapped out with something similar and it will still taste good. Use Greek yogurt instead of regular. Almond milk instead of milk. Maple syrup or even no sweetener instead of honey.

Prepare the mason jars. Fill them with a handful of whatever special ingredients you want. Then top them off with the oatmeal mixture.

Place them in the fridge and enjoy a healthy breakfast or snack throughout the day!
