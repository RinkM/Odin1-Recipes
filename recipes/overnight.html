Morning After Oats

Makes 10 Mason Jars

Base Recipe:

  • 2.5 cups oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 3.25 cup milk
  • 2.5 cup yogurt

Apple Peanut Butter

  1. 1 Apple, diced
  2. 1 Tb Peanut Butter

Blueberry Coconut Lemon

  1. 2 oz blueberries
  2. 1 Tb Coconut Shavings
  3. 1 tsp Lemon Juice

Berry Mix

  1. 2 ounces of your favorite berries

Strawberry Banana

  1. 1 or 2 strawberries, sliced
  2. 1 spoon of banana, sliced
